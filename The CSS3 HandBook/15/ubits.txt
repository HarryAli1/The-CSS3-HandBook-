                        Real-world measurement units(useful for printing)
1.cm   (maps to 37.8pixels)
2.mm   (0.1cm)
3.q    (a quarter of a millimeter)
4.in   inch(maps to 96 pixels)
5.pt   point(1 inch = 72 points)
6.pc   pica(1 pica=12 points)



                        Relative Units

1.em    (assined to this element's font-size ---the width of the 'm' letter)---only font-size can change its value


2.rem   (assined to the font-size of the root('html') element )---- nothing but root's font-size can change it 


3.ex    (assinged to this element's font-size ----the height of the 'x' letter) -----font-size , font-family, font-weight


4.ch    (assinged to this element's font-size ----the height of 0(zero)) -----font-size , font-family, font-weight
